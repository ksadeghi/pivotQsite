Executive Summary

IaC Trans helps organizations move cloud and AI initiatives into production faster by converting Infrastructure as Code (IaC) into decision-ready security, cost, and compliance guidance—before deployment.

  • Reduce risk and rework with consistent assessments aligned to recognized industry standards
  • Improve cost control by forecasting spend early and identifying optimization opportunities
  • Increase audit readiness through clear documentation, traceability, and evidence generation
Up to 95% reduction in manual assessment effort through standardization and automation
20–40% potential cost optimization opportunities identified early via design-time guidance
Faster approvals and fewer rework cycles by shifting governance left (before deployment)
Improved audit readiness with consistent evidence, traceability, and documentation quality
The Challenge
Recurring delivery friction
  • Manual, inconsistent reviews slow delivery and trigger late-stage redesign
  • Late cost discovery leads to budget surprises and inefficient architectures
  • High compliance overhead increases audit burden and operational risk
Limited visibility and scale
  • Stale architecture artifacts reduce visibility into data flows and trust boundaries
  • Slow DevSecOps feedback loops limit scale across teams and environments
What IaC Trans Delivers
1) Security & Risk Clarity
  • Threat modeling and risk scoring with clear rationale
  • Prioritized mitigation plan (“fix first / fix next / monitor”)
  • Optional mapping to common standards (e.g., SOC 2, NIST; others as required)
2) Cost & FinOps Insight
  • Service-level cost estimates with transparent assumptions
  • Scenario planning (low / expected / high) to support budgeting
  • Optimization recommendations (quick wins + structural improvements)
3) Architecture Visibility
  • Current-state diagrams and data flow views
  • Clear security boundaries and compliance zones
  • Artifacts suitable for reviews, audits, and cross-team handoffs
How It Works
Structured workflow
  1. Ingest IaC and environment assumptions
  2. Assess across security, architecture, and cost dimensions
  3. Score and explain tradeoffs in business and technical terms
  4. Output prioritized actions plus audit-ready documentation
  5. Support CI/CD integration so teams receive actionable feedback before changes reach production
Aligned to recognized standards

Designed to align with recognized practices (e.g., AWS Well-Architected and common threat modeling standards) while remaining adaptable to your internal governance model.

Note: IaC Trans can be configured to map outputs to the controls and evidence formats your organization already uses.

Why IaC Trans Enables Production AI

AI increases the stakes: sensitive data access, complex permissions, faster iteration cycles, and volatile compute spend. IaC Trans makes governance repeatable, fast, and measurable, enabling teams to deploy AI with confidence—while maintaining security posture, compliance integrity, and cost control at scale.
